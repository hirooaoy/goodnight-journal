# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 15, 2026**

## Introduction

Welcome to Goodnight Journal. We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and handle your personal information when you use our journaling application.

## Our Privacy Commitment

**Your journal entries are private and belong to you.** We do not read, share, sell, or use your journal content for any purpose other than providing you with the service.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Account Information
When you sign up using Apple Sign In or Google Sign In, we collect:
- Your email address
- Your name (if provided by the authentication service)
- A unique user identifier from the authentication provider

### 2. Journal Content
- Journal entries you create
- Timestamps of when entries were created or modified
- Any metadata associated with your entries

### 3. Technical Information
- Device information (device type, operating system version)
- App usage analytics (crash reports, performance data)
- IP address (for security purposes only)

### 4. Audio Usage
- We do not record or store any audio from your device
- Ambient sounds (fireplace, etc.) are played locally on your device

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:
- Provide and maintain the App's functionality
- Sync your journal entries across your devices
- Authenticate your identity and secure your account
- Improve the App's performance and fix bugs
- Send important service updates (if necessary)

**We do NOT:**
- Read your journal entries
- Share your personal information with third parties for marketing
- Sell your data to anyone
- Use your journal content to train AI models or for any other purpose

## Data Storage and Security

### Security Measures
- All data is encrypted in transit using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols
- Journal entries are stored securely in Firebase Firestore with encryption at rest
- Authentication is handled by trusted providers (Apple, Google)
- Access to your data is restricted to your account only

### Data Location
- Your data is stored on secure Firebase servers
- Firebase complies with major data protection regulations including GDPR and CCPA

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT share your personal information with third parties except:
- **With your explicit consent**
- **For legal compliance:** If required by law or to respond to legal processes
- **For service providers:** Firebase (Google) hosts our backend services and has its own privacy commitments

We will never sell your data or use it for advertising purposes.

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:
- **Access your data:** View all journal entries within the App
- **Export your data:** Request a copy of your journal entries
- **Delete your data:** Delete individual entries or your entire account
- **Opt-out of analytics:** Contact us to disable analytics collection

### Account Deletion
To delete your account and all associated data:
1. Go to the App settings
2. Select "Delete Account"
3. Confirm deletion

Please note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

## Third-Party Services

### Authentication Providers
- **Apple Sign In:** Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy
- **Google Sign In:** Subject to Google's Privacy Policy

### Backend Services
- **Firebase/Firestore:** Subject to Google's Privacy Policy and Firebase's security commitments

We recommend reviewing these third-party privacy policies as well.

## Data Retention

- **Active accounts:** We retain your data for as long as your account is active
- **Deleted accounts:** Upon account deletion, your data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days
- **Inactive accounts:** We may delete accounts that have been inactive for more than 2 years after providing notice

## International Users

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States and other countries where our service providers operate.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:
- Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top
- Posting a notice in the App
- Sending an email notification (if we have your email address)

Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights:
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt-out of sale of personal information (we don't sell your data)
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

## European Users (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have rights under GDPR:
- Right to access your personal data
- Right to rectification of inaccurate data
- Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing
- Right to withdraw consent

## Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

- **Email:** privacy@goodnightjournal.app
- **Response time:** We aim to respond within 48 hours

## Transparency

We believe in radical transparency about privacy:
- We don't use your journal content for any purpose
- We don't train AI on your entries
- We don't share your data with advertisers
- Your journals are yours, period
